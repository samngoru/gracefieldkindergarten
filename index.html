Why Gracefield?

We're located along Amboseli Road in the Lavington suburb of Nairobi and our kindergarten is set in a large compound that is serene and secure with plenty of space to learn and play. We treat each child as an individual, nurturing their interests and talents.
Our mission is to provide a quality, holistic education that lays the right foundation for your child to achieve their full potential. We strive to provide our services with passion, professionalism and integrity.

Our Philosophy

To ensure our children are prepared to qualify for enrollment at all leading schools in Kenya and beyond.

Closely Monitored Learning

Each child is given an opportunity to learn at their pace and their progress monitored closely to reinforce learning

Conducive Learning Environment

Our learning environment is always friendly, fun, engaging, yet challenging.

Well Rounded Curriculum

We provide an all inclusive curriculum of classroom activities to build knowledge and extra-curricular activities like swimming, music, pottery, ballet, tennis and horse riding that build motor skills, and allow children to express themselves in ways that develop them
